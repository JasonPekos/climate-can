Climate Data

We have the following parameters in the climate dataset:

  • Longitude..x., Latitude..y.
  • Station.Name
  • Climate.ID
  • Date.Time (Year, Month)
  • Mean.Max.Temp…C.
  • Mean.Max.Temp.Flag

Just lat, lon.

Gives name of weather station. See also: Date.Time.
